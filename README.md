# 💎 BillingPro | Next-Gen POS & Inventory Engine

**BillingPro** is a premium, high-performance Point of Sale (POS) and Inventory Management system engineered for modern retailers. Built with **Next.js 15**, it combines enterprise-grade functionality with a sleek, app-like user interface that works seamlessly on mobile and desktop.

## 🌟 Core Modules

### � Intelligent Terminal (Billing)

- **Optical Scan Engine**: High-speed barcode scanning using deep-lens camera integration.
- **Dynamic Cart**: Real-time tax (GST) calculation with support for inclusive/exclusive pricing.
- **Bill Finalization**: Instant digital previews and localized receipt formatting.

### 📦 Master Stock Room (Inventory)

- **Hierarchical Management**: Detailed product tracking with names, prices, and live stock quantities.
- **Low Stock Intelligence**: Visual indicators and alerts for items running low.
- **Bulk Operations**: Seamlessly import/export inventory registries via Excel/CSV.
- **Global Search**: Instant lookup across thousands of SKUs with zero lag.

### 📊 Business Analytics

- **Live Revenue Tracking**: Real-time progress monitoring against daily sales targets.
- **Transaction History**: Searchable audit log of every bill generated.
- **Asset Valuation**: Automated calculation of total stock value.

## �️ Technical Architecture

- **Frontend**: `Next.js 15` (App Router) for hybrid rendering.
- **Style System**: `Vanilla CSS` with custom Design Tokens (Glassmorphism UI).
- **Motion**: `Framer Motion` for fluid structural transitions.
- **Data Vault**: `Dexie.js` / `IndexedDB` for secure, offline-first data persistence.
- **Hardware Bridge**: `Bluetooth GATT` for thermal printers and `html5-qrcode` for scanners.

## 🛡️ Privacy & Ghost Protocol

BillingPro operates on the **Ghost Protocol**. No business data, customer information, or sales logs are ever transmitted to external servers. All data remains within your browser's encrypted local vault.
